Member Exclusive Services

Club members have access to several unique services!

Stringing Service

Choose your string, choose your tension, and we will get your your racket strung ASAP! The Stringing Service is provided to us via JZone Badminton, if you would like to browse the selection strings they have, check out: www.jzonebadminton.com

We also do custom stencilling!

Note: There is a delivery fee of $3 per racket.

Order Through Us!

Can't make the commute to Jzone in person? No problem! The UTSCBC will order the racket you want using the JZone membership discount. Tell us the racket or item you would like to purchase, (along with string/tension if needed) and we will get you your item! See our club table for further inquiries.

Coaching / Seminars

At the Advanced Skills Seminars players will have the opportunity to play and learn from national level badminton athletes and Varsity Blues Players! The seminars will be held and coached by UTSCBC's and Varsity Blues' Jonathan Lai.​

The seminars will encompass an in-depth look at many techniques, tricks, and skills of badminton, players of all skill levels are welcome to attend! We HIGHLY encourage beginner and intermediate level players to sign up!
