About Us

Play to live and live to play

We are a club dedicated to providing the best experience for badminton players like ourselves at the University of Toronto Scarborough (UTSC). We strive to create a community where everyone of all skill levels are welcome to play at our club hours or drop-in hours at the Toronto Pan Am Sports Center (TPASC). Most importantly, we want everyone to have fun and enjoy badminton as much as we do!
