Club Sponsors

The UTSC BC has joined forces with some leading Badminton Brands, Providers, and Clubs! Check them out below and see how your membership can get you bonuses and discounts!

JZone Badminton

Jzone Badminton is a legitimate YONEX Authorized Badminton Pro Shop located at the Langham Square Mall (Kennedy & Hwy7) They provide a massive variety of high end, professional and high quality YONEX racquets, shoes, grips, bags, and badminton equipment that would be tough to find at larger commercial retailers. Members receive a 10% discount on all regular price items with exception of shuttles.

*DISCOUNT REDEAMABLE IN PERSON ONLY*

Check out Jzone!

Li-Ning

Li-Ning is a professional badminton equipment brand and is a world leader in badminton racket production with numerous world and Olympic badminton champions using their products including Chen Long , Liliyana Natsir, Tontowi Ahmad, Zhang Nan and many more. In addition to their amazing military grade carbon fiber badminton rackets, Li-Ning offers cutting edge badminton shoe designs and a huge assortment of shuttlecocks and badminton string Visit your local dealer or shop online with FREE SHIPPING on orders $150 or more.

Check out Li-Ning!

Commander Badminton Club

The club is managed by a group of individuals who have great passion of the badminton game. At Commander they believe badminton club is a platform where one can meet friends, keep fitness and have fun. They ensure all club players to enjoy their game and have fun! Whether you are seasonal or professional badminton player, all are welcome to join the club and they commit to providing best of the facility and services to all of club members and guest. They offer several programs and practices to offer players of all levels and all ages.

UTSCBC Members can get $3 discounted off of the drop-in price by presenting their membership card at the counter.

*DISCOUNT REDEAMABLE IN PERSON ONLY*
