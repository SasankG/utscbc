Club Drop-In

Club hours are presented to UTSCBC members and membership card will be checked upon entry. Nylon birdies and extra rackets will be provided by a present UTSCBC staff member.

There are also drop-in hours for badminton hosted by the Toronto Pan Am Sports Center! Everyone is encouraged to participate in those hours as well as club hours! ​ Note that there are times specifically for UTSC students and for the general public (called the All-access-drop in). UTSC students CAN attend hours that are for the general public at no extra cost.
